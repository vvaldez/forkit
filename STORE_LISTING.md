### Description (4000 chars max)

Fork delivery apps. Cook it yourself — and feel great about it.

ForkIt is a meal planning app built on a single premise: the money you spend on delivery is money you could pocket. We borrowed the slick UX you love from food delivery apps — browse, tap, add to cart, checkout — but instead of a driver showing up with a bag, you walk away with a grocery list and a plan.

**Discover what's for dinner**
Browse hundreds of recipes organized by meal type, cuisine, and what's in your fridge. Hit "Surprise me" when decision fatigue strikes and let ForkIt pick for you. The questionnaire narrows it down by cook time, dietary preference, and what you've already eaten this week.

**Stop repeating the same five meals**
ForkIt actively steers you toward new dishes. Recipes you've cooked recently get pushed down so you discover something different every week.

**Your grocery list, built for you**
Add recipes to your cart, tap Checkout, and get a combined grocery list — or break it down by meal. Check off items as you shop. Done.

**See the savings add up**
Every meal you cook at home is savings vs. delivery. ForkIt estimates what you saved and keeps a running lifetime total. The number gets satisfying fast.

**Your recipes, your data**
Save favorites, write custom recipes, track what you've cooked, and log leftovers. Everything lives on your device — no account required for any core feature. Sign in only when you want to share a recipe with the community.

**Offline-first**
Browse, plan, shop, and cook — no internet required. ForkIt works completely offline.

**Community recipes**
Browse recipes created and shared by other ForkIt cooks. Save any recipe to your collection. Share your own with one tap.

### Full description (4000 chars max)

Fork delivery apps. Cook it yourself.

ForkIt is a meal planning app that turns the delivery model on its head. Same slick browse-and-checkout UX — but instead of placing an order, you walk away with a grocery list and a plan to cook it yourself.

DISCOVER WHAT'S FOR DINNER
Browse hundreds of recipes by meal type, cuisine, and dietary preference. Tap "Surprise me" for an instant pick, or use the questionnaire to filter by cook time and what you haven't cooked lately.

STOP REPEATING THE SAME MEALS
ForkIt tracks your meal history and steers you toward new dishes. Recipes you've cooked in the last 7 days get de-prioritized automatically.

GROCERY LIST IN TWO TAPS
Add recipes to your cart → Checkout → get a complete grocery list. View it combined or broken down by meal. Check items off as you shop.

TRACK YOUR SAVINGS
Every home-cooked meal is money saved vs. delivery. ForkIt estimates your savings per checkout and keeps a running lifetime total.

OFFLINE FIRST
Browse, plan, shop, and cook without an internet connection. Everything that matters works offline.

YOUR DATA, YOUR DEVICE
Favorites, custom recipes, meal history, leftovers, and your cart all live on your device. No account required for any core feature.

COMMUNITY RECIPES
Browse recipes shared by other ForkIt cooks. Save any recipe to your collection. Share your own in one tap.
