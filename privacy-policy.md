# ForkIt Privacy Policy

**Last updated: 2026-04-18**

## What we collect

**Email address** — collected only when you choose to sign in. Used solely to authenticate your account via Supabase. Never sold or shared with third parties.

**Recipe data you create** — custom recipes, favorites, meal history, and grocery lists are stored locally on your device. They are not transmitted to any server unless you explicitly share a recipe to the community.

**Community recipes** — if you choose to share a recipe, it is published to a shared Supabase database along with your email address as the author display name. Published recipes are visible to all app users.

## What we do not collect

- Location data
- Device identifiers or advertising IDs
- Analytics or usage telemetry
- Contacts or calendar data
- Any data from third-party sources

## Data storage

- **On-device:** All recipe, cart, history, and settings data is stored in a local SQLite database on your device. Uninstalling the app removes this data.
- **Supabase:** Account sessions are stored encrypted on your device using the secure keychain. Community recipes are stored on a Supabase-hosted database.

## Data deletion

To delete your account and any community recipes you have published, email vinny.valdez@gmail.com with the subject "Delete my ForkIt account." We will remove your data within 30 days.

## Third-party services

- **Supabase** — authentication and community recipe storage. [Supabase Privacy Policy](https://supabase.com/privacy)
- **TheMealDB** — bundled recipe data is sourced from TheMealDB and is read-only within the app.
